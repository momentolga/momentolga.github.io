CONTACT

My name is Olga. I am a photographer based in Switzerland, passionate about art and communication. Here I have created a gallery where you can discover my works in different genres. Welcome!

Behind every inspiring picture lies a story and I will be thrilled to help you telling it. The most important thing about a picture is revealing the correct mood and message so it is emotionally visible to the viewers.

Believe me, together we will enjoy the three stages of a photography session.

1. At the preparation stage we will discuss very important details such as:
- style and ambience,
- props,
- location,
- best lightening conditions.
2. During the session we will have a wonderful time where I will help you in posing.
3. You will choose the best images for editing and receive the link to your fantastic memories.

I am always open for cooperation and new projects in any corner of the world.

For further information or to book a photo session, please contact me by email, instagram or facebook.
